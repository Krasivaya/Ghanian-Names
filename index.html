About

Frequently in Ghana, both boys and girls are given their first name depending on the day of the week they were born on.

Most of the times there are refered as 'Akan Names', and girls and boys get different names depending on their gender and the day of the week they were born.

Example:

I am a boy, and I was born on Sunday. My Ghanian name is Kwasi.

I am a girl, and I was born on Sunday. My Ghanian name is Akosua.

Most of people don't know the days of the week they were born on. Don't worry we have a way to help you know that day and generate for you a Ghanian Name.
